I have the pleasure of riding with Kyle, Sawyer, and Henri on a weekly basis, and this is their brainchild. I have a segment in the film, and since a ski film is a full season endeavor, I'm often riding with them throughout; helping scout features, riding along, or just getting to be part of the fun. Being part of what my friends create is a true pleasure, and seeing it all come together is even better. Here, I get to be part of my friends' vision, and for every scene I get to see the whole day of adventure that went into getting it. When your friends ask you to get outside, you probably should.

**The Idea**  
What really is paradise? A distant dreamland of endless snow, or your home mountain on a perfect powder day? Maybe paradise isn't a place at all — maybe it's the friends and strangers alike who turn those snowy days into something magical. Whatever it is, we hope you find yours.
